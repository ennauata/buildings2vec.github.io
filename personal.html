    Real Me : )

    I used to live in Beijing, from 2 to 22 years old (That's soooo long). But I love the food, the places and all my friends here. I will miss the city more if smog getting better controlled haha.

    I enjoy the life in Vancouver, in particular, the temperature, the plants, the hiking trails. Well, mostly, I am crazy with skiing and Vancouver is the holy land for playing that (yeah!).
